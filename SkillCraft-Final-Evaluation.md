**Q1: "I see you built a Meeting-to-JSON converter. How did you stop the AI from generating conversational text like 'Here is your JSON'?"**
**Model Answer:** "That's a classic issue that breaks automated parsing scripts. I solved it using explicit negative constraints. In the system prompt, I wrote: 'Output ONLY the raw, valid JSON. **Q2: "In your AI Interview Assistant, what do you mean by a 'State Machine' prompt?"**
**Model Answer:** "LLMs are natively designed to be as helpful as possible in a single turn, which causes them to 'rush' conversational workflows. To fix this, I defined specific states in the prompt (e.g., State 1: Icebreaker, State 2: Behavioral Question) and strictly commanded the AI to never proceed to the next state until the user responded. This effectively held the AI back, forcing it to act as an interactive agent navigating a loop, rather than just generating a wall of text."
